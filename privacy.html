SMS Opt-In and Consent Data

We will not share your opt-in to an SMS campaign with any third party for purposes unrelated to providing you with the services of that campaign. We may share your Personal Data, including your SMS opt-in or consent status, with third parties that help us provide our messaging services, including but not limited to platform providers, phone companies, and any other vendors who assist us in the delivery of text messages. All of the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Information We Collect

Advantage Rehabilitation & Wellness Center may collect personal information you provide, including your name, phone number, email address, appointment request details, health-related visit reason, communication preferences, and SMS opt-in or consent status.

How We Use Information

We use your information to schedule appointments, respond to requests, provide chiropractic and wellness services, send service-related communications, deliver SMS messages you have consented to receive, improve our services, and comply with legal or regulatory obligations.

Service Providers

We may share limited personal information with vendors and service providers who help us operate our office, manage scheduling, host our website, process communications, and deliver requested services. These providers are expected to use the information only to perform services for us.

Your Choices

You may contact us to update your information or communication preferences. For SMS messages, you may reply STOP to unsubscribe or HELP for help. Message and data rates may apply.
